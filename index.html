About Albayan Nikah Association

Albayan Nikah Association facilitates Islamic marriages in a respectful, organized, and Islamically sound manner. Nikah is performed by a licensed marriage officer in accordance with Islamic principles and Ontario marriage regulations. We provide Nikah services in Brampton, Mississauga, Toronto, and across Ontario.

Imam Profile

Imam El-Husseini Anas

Imam at Masjid Al Salam, Brampton

Holding a university degree from Al-Azhar University and Ijazah in Qur’an and Islamic sciences.

Imam El-Husseini Anas serves the Muslim community with dedication and integrity, helping couples complete their Nikah in accordance with the Qur’an and Sunnah while ensuring the process is organized and respectful for families.

Nikah Requirements

  • Consent of both bride and groom
  • Two Muslim witnesses
  • Wali (guardian) for the bride
  • Agreed mahr (dowry)
  • Valid identification documents

How to Book a Nikah

  • Step 1: Fill out the Nikah request form with your details and preferred date.
  • Step 2: We will contact you to confirm the details and answer any questions.
  • Step 3: The Nikah ceremony will be conducted at the agreed location in accordance with the Qur’an and Sunnah.
