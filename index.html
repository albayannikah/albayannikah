About Albayan Nikah Association

Albayan Nikah Association facilitates Islamic marriages in a respectful, organized and Islamically sound manner. Nikah is performed by a licensed marriage officer in accordance with Islamic principles and Ontario marriage regulations.

Nikah Services

Nikah Ceremony

Conducting Islamic marriage ceremonies in accordance with the Qur’an and Sunnah.

Marriage Guidance

Helping couples understand Islamic marriage requirements.

Community Support

Helping families organize a simple and respectful Nikah ceremony.

Nikah Requirements

  • Consent of both bride and groom
  • Two Muslim witnesses
  • Wali (guardian) for the bride
  • Agreed mahr (dowry)
  • Valid identification documents
